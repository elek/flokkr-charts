Apache Hadoop HDFS-7240 up and running.


You can access the components with


1. PORT forwarding

export SCM_POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app={{ template "flokkr.fullname" . }},component=scm" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
kubectl port-forward $SCM_POD_NAME 9876:9876
export KSM_POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app={{ template "flokkr.fullname" . }},component=ksm" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
kubectl port-forward $KSM_POD_NAME 9874:9874


2. kubectl proxy

kubectl proxy

http://localhost:8001/api/v1/namespaces/{{ .Release.Namespace }}/services/{{ template "flokkr.fullname" . }}-ksm:web/proxy/
http://localhost:8001/api/v1/namespaces/{{ .Release.Namespace }}/services/{{ template "flokkr.fullname" . }}-scm:web/proxy/
